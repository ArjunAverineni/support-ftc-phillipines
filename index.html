Our Mission

Our mission is to ignite a passion for STEM and robotics among students at Our Lady of Joy Learning Center in the Philippines by establishing the first-ever FTC team in the country. Through our outreach, we have already introduced hundreds of elementary, middle, and high school students to hands-on robotics and the values of FIRST. With your support, we will build on this momentum by providing resources, mentorship, and opportunities that empower these students to compete, innovate, and inspire their communities through robotics. Together, we can open doors for the next generation of Filipino engineers, programmers, and changemakers.

Project Phillipines

Project Philippines is our initiative to launch the first-ever FTC robotics team at Our Lady of Joy Learning Center. After successfully teaching robotics to elementary, middle, and high school students during our outreach trip, we saw a powerful opportunity to create lasting impact. With your support, we aim to provide the resources, mentorship, and equipment needed to build a competitive and sustainable FTC team in the Philippines—giving students access to world-class STEM education and global opportunities.

FAQ

What resources are provided?

Participating schools often receive robotics kits, technical guidance, training workshops, and access to competition resources to support student engagement in STEM.


How can I help beyond donating?

You can make a huge difference by running a mini fundraising campaign among friends and family, and by sharing the website on social media or in local groups to boost visibility. Every share creates new opportunities for support and helps spread the mission further.

About Us

We are FTC Team Tomahawk #17315 from Westwood Robotics, a 15-member team with a passion for innovation and impact. We've qualified for the FTC World Championship twice and were proud World Finalists in 2024, earning awards like Winning Alliance Captain, Control Award, and 2nd Place Motivate. Beyond competing, we focus on outreach—mentoring teams and teaching robotics worldwide. Our newest goal: building the first-ever FTC team in the Philippines at Our Lady of Joy Learning Center.
